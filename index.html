Biography

I am a researcher and engineer in Artificial Intelligence with mixed academia and industry experiences for about 10 years. My background covers the fields of deep learning, computer vision, medical image analysis and recommendation. Currently, I am a Research Associate at ÉTS Montreal. Besides technical stuff, I have passion in reading, travelling and sports.
